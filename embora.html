Our Belief

Bold Ideas, Brought to Life

We combine perception, cognition, and action to create AI systems that understand and interact with the physical world. From robotic foundations to human-centered interfaces, we build intelligence that lives in reality.

融合感知、认知与行动，打造理解物理世界、与现实交互的人工智能系统。大胆构想，赋予生命。

Research Focus

Core research directions in embodied intelligence

Perception

感知

Multi-modal · Vision

Multi-modal sensing systems that see, hear, touch, and understand spatial relationships in real time. 多模态传感系统，实时感知视觉、听觉、触觉与空间关系。

Cognition

认知

Reasoning · Planning

Neural architectures for reasoning, planning, and decision-making in dynamic, uncertain environments. 面向动态不确定环境的推理、规划与决策神经架构。

Action

行动

Robotics · Control

Closed-loop control systems that translate high-level intent into precise physical motion. 将高层意图转化为精确物理运动的闭环控制系统。

Evolution

进化

Learning · Adaptation

Self-improving agents that learn from every interaction and adapt to new tasks autonomously. 从每次交互中学习、自主适应新任务的自进化智能体。
