Our Vision / 我们的愿景

From digital minds
to physical presence

We believe the next leap in artificial intelligence isn't just about better language models or bigger datasets. It's about giving AI a body — the ability to touch, move, and interact with the physical world the way humans do. Embora exists to make this transition happen.

我们相信，人工智能的下一步飞跃不只是更好的语言模型或更大的数据集， 而是赋予 AI 一个"身体"——让它能够像人类一样触摸、移动、与物理世界互动。 Embora 存在的意义，就是推动这一转变的发生。

Core Research / 核心研究

Three pillars of embodied intelligence

◉

Perception

感知

Multi-modal sensing systems that enable machines to see, hear, and feel their environment with human-level fidelity. 多模态感知系统，让机器以人类级别的精度观察、聆听和感知周围环境。

◆

Cognition

认知

Reasoning engines that understand physics, spatial relationships, and causal logic to make intelligent decisions. 推理引擎，理解物理规律、空间关系和因果逻辑，做出智能决策。

△

Action

行动

Execution systems that translate intelligence into precise physical interaction and manipulation. 将智能转化为精准物理交互和操作的执行系统。

Roadmap / 发展路线

From foundation to reality

Phase 01 — 2026

Foundation Research

基础研究阶段

Establish core research in multi-modal perception and world models. Build our founding team and secure initial funding. 开展多模态感知与世界模型的核心研究，组建创始团队并获得初始资金。

Phase 02 — 2027

Proto-Agent

原型智能体

Develop our first embodied AI agent capable of navigating and interacting within controlled environments. 开发首个能在受控环境中导航和交互的具身 AI 智能体。

Phase 03 — 2028

Real-World Deployment

真实世界部署

Deploy embodied agents in real-world scenarios — from smart manufacturing to assistive robotics. 将具身智能体部署到真实场景——从智能制造到辅助机器人。

Phase 04 — Beyond

General Embodied Intelligence

通用具身智能

Pursue general-purpose embodied AI that can learn and adapt across any physical task, in any environment. 追求通用具身智能——能在任何物理任务和环境中学习与适应。
