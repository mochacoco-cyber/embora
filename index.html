Our Vision · 我们的愿景

From digital minds to physical presence

The next leap in AI isn't about better language models or bigger datasets. It's about giving intelligence a body — the ability to touch, move, and interact with the physical world the way humans do.

AI 的下一步飞跃不是更好的语言模型，而是赋予智能一个"身体"——让它能够触摸、移动、与物理世界互动。

Core Research · 核心研究

Three pillars of embodied intelligence

  • 01

    Perception

    感知

    Multi-modal sensing systems that enable machines to see, hear, and feel their environment with human-level fidelity. 多模态感知系统，让机器以人类精度观察、聆听和感知周围环境。

  • 02

    Cognition

    认知

    Reasoning engines that understand physics, spatial relationships, and causal logic to make intelligent decisions. 推理引擎，理解物理规律、空间关系和因果逻辑，做出智能决策。

  • 03

    Action

    行动

    Execution systems that translate intelligence into precise physical interaction and manipulation. 将智能转化为精准物理交互和操作的执行系统。

Roadmap · 发展路线

From foundation to reality

Phase 01 · 2026

Foundation Research

基础研究阶段

Establish core research in multi-modal perception and world models. Build the founding team. 开展多模态感知与世界模型的核心研究，组建创始团队。

Phase 02 · 2027

Proto-Agent

原型智能体

Develop the first embodied AI agent capable of navigating and interacting within controlled environments. 开发首个能在受控环境中导航和交互的具身 AI 智能体。

Phase 03 · 2028

Real-World Deployment

真实世界部署

Deploy agents in real-world scenarios — from smart manufacturing to assistive robotics. 将具身智能体部署到真实场景——从智能制造到辅助机器人。

Phase 04 · Beyond

General Embodied Intelligence

通用具身智能

Pursue general-purpose embodied AI that learns and adapts across any physical task, in any environment. 追求能在任何物理任务和环境中学习与适应的通用具身智能。
